Welcome to TeacherAsked

This app will allow a teacher (that has been logged before) to create a room with a ID and to give it to his students. The students can connect to the room with a given username and once the students are in the room, the teacher can asked a question that will be display to all the student. Before the student has gave a response to the question, he can't see other's response. When he validate his response, all the responses already gave will be display to him. He can't change his answer anymore. The teacher can always see all the responses in real time but can also choose to hide them for the moment (If his screen is displaying in front of the class for exemple). When the teacher decide to close the question, some statistics will be display to him and he can create a new question.

Getting started

Are you a teacher or a student?

Login page

You are a teacher

  1. Go on the application by clicking on the "Go to the app" button or by following this link.
  2. Log in with your credentials or register by filling the 4 fields.
  3. Once you are on your account page, you can either create a new room or see your old questions by clicking on them.
    4. Teacher profile page

  4. Once you are in a new room, you can see the students that are connected and ask a new question. Once a question is asked, you have the possibility to see the result in real time on your page or not (uncheck the checkbox if your screen is display in front of the students). Once the everybody has answer the question, you can close the question and a lot of statistics will be display for you and your students. At this point, you can either close the room or begin a new question in the same room.
    5. Teacher ask a new question

    Teacher asked a question

You are a student

  1. Go on the application by clicking on the "Go to the app" button or by following this link.
  2. Log in the room by entering an username and the room ID that your teacher gave you.
  3. Once you are in the room, you can see the other students that are connected and the current question that is asked (if applicable). You can answer the question by filling the input that is show to you or the multiple choice list. You can't see others answer for the moment.
    4. Inside a new room

  4. Once the question is close by your teacher, you can see others answers and a lot of statistics about it if there is an auto-check.
